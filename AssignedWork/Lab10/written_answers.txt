The pivot is essentially the dividing line that splits the array into smaller subarrays.
A well-chosen pivote creates balanced subarrays that can be solved efficiently in parallel recursive calls while a poor one can create skewed subarrays 
that don't take full advantage of the divide-and-conquer benefit.
